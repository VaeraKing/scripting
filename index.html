Vaera King

About

Vaera King is a fist year student at the Lethbridge Polytechnic, taking Multimedia Production. On her own she is a sports and lifestyle photographer; Vaera.esther_media, self-taught over the last 5 years. She has previously completed the “Intro to Photography and Photoshop” course through the Polytechnic's Digital Communications Program through a high school dual credit opportunity. She decided to enroll in this course based on her desire to stay close to home (only an hour and a half away) while learning about something she is passionate about. While she was deciding what to go into after high school her mama encouraged her to take a course that would bring out creativity and be about something she loves. She has done lots of wildlife photography and videography while on hunting trips with her dad and all around where she has grown up in southern AB. She got her first camera in 2019 and immediately wanted to learn and develop her skills as much as she could. An eighteen-year-old farm girl who grew up with a love for God's creation and a desire to capture its beauty. She has been busy taking lots of family and graduation photo sessions, as well as being at all her siblings sports capturing the action furthering her entrepreneurship. Hoping this course will give her more knowledge and the capability to further her career into digital design and the better ability to produce high quality content.
