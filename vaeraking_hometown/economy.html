Economy

Most people in the community are farmers or ranchers. However, we do have a vast number of other occupations required, such as bankers, grocers, teachers, coaches, and so many more. The town has two banks, one grocery store, a K-12 school, several restaurants, and plenty of sports teams. There is no shortage of job opportunities here! The village also has recently had the windmill operations through several times which has brought lots of job opportunities and revenue to our community, as much as most farmers would complain about the eyesore.
