Foremost AB

Geography

Foremost is located in southeast Alberta Canada. 106 km southwest of Medicine Hat, 110 km east of Lethbridge, along the Red Coat Trail, in the County of Forty Mile No. 8. It is built on native prairie grass pastures surrounded by farmland. A view of the beautiful Sweet Grass Hills on the horizon and endless sky never gets old. Being so far in the middle of nowhere gives it a personal and safe feeling while only being a one-hour drive to two of the provinces' larger cities also gives you the comfort of not being too far from civilization.
